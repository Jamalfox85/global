Need help finding us?

How do you find us?

If you're parking, we recommend parking at the parking lot right off of Airline Rd. This parking lot belongs to the apartment buildinds next door but non-residents are able to park here for a fee. Alternatively, you can park on the side of Airline Rd. for free. Be sure not to block any driveways as the residents will need to be able to leave and come home as they please. Violators may be towed.

For Uber/Lift, you can instruct your drivers to drive you to 'Edge Apartments'. Right next to this apartment complex is a short path that will lead you to the beltline. As soon as you enter the beltline, turn left (away from the Shake Shack) and follow that down. We are located at the very end of the first shopping center you will encounter on your left.
